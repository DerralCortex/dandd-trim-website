About Us

A Family-Built Legacy

Since 2004, D and D Trim and Cabinets has been dedicated to providing top-notch finish carpentry services across the Inland Northwest. We specialize in the installation of doors, baseboards, mantles, window treatments, and a wide variety of millwork applications.

From production work for major builders like Greenstone and Lennar to customized residential projects, we take pride in meticulous craftsmanship. Whether it's cabinets or precision trim — we strive for excellence in every detail.

Quality and timeliness are our top priorities on every project, and we never compromise on safety for our team.
